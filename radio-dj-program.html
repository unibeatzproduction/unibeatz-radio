DJ Types

Guest DJ

Entry-level DJs for community growth, testing talent, special mixes, and event appearances.

Resident DJ

Official recurring Uni Radio DJs with scheduled shows, profile placement, and future paid contract eligibility.

Premium DJ

Top-tier DJs for prime events, sponsor opportunities, and higher performance-based earning potential.

Application System

  • DJ name and real name
  • Email and social links
  • Genres and city/state
  • Experience and availability
  • Bio and sample mix upload

Flow: Applied → Under Review → Approved → Guest DJ → Resident DJ → Premium DJ.

Performance Tracking

  • Live listeners
  • Replay listeners
  • Average session time
  • Returning listeners
  • Engagement
  • Genre performance

Ranks: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Legend.

Pay Structure

Startup: tips, exposure, profile building, event opportunities.

Growth: stipends, show bonuses, sponsorship percentages.

10K+ consistent listeners: Resident DJ target = $10K/year base contract, with bonuses and sponsorship upside.
